What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new and original content‚Äîsuch as text, images, music, video, code, and even 3D models‚Äîbased on the patterns it learns from large amounts of data. Unlike traditional AI, which focuses on analyzing information and making predictions, generative AI is designed to produce something new. These models learn from examples, understand underlying structures, and then generate outputs that are coherent, creative, and human-like. This allows them to write stories, design graphics, compose music, build software, simulate environments, and assist humans across countless industries.
